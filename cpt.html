Gain Experience While You Study

Curricular Practical Training (CPT) is work authorization for eligible F-1 students to pursue paid or unpaid off-campus internships that are part of their curriculum.

What is CPT?

CPT allows F-1 students to work off-campus in a role directly related to their major. It must be an integral part of your curriculum and approved by OISS before you begin.

  • Requires a job/internship offer
  • Must be employer- and date-specific
  • Applies to both paid and unpaid roles

Terra Dotta CPT Process – Step-by-Step

Step 1: Login to Terra Dotta

Go to the Terra Dotta portal: https://eiu-isss.terradotta.com/_portal/ and select “New or Current Student.” Login using your EIU credentials.

Step 2: Access Control Center

Once logged in, click on the Control Center from the dashboard. This is where all student actions and requests are managed.

Step 3: Select CPT Request

Scroll down the Control Center page and locate the “CPT Request” widget on the right side. Click it to begin the process.

Step 4: Fill CPT Questionnaire

A new window will pop up with the CPT questionnaire. Carefully fill in your internship details and academic connections. Then click Next to proceed.
